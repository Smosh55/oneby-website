# OneBy

**The AI Communications OS that turns every call into action.**

OneBy is a full business communications suite (calling, desk phones with auto-provisioning, business SMS, online fax, and a mobile app) with an AI layer that runs on every call. We do not sell phones and minutes. We sell the outcome: a resolved follow-up. After every call, answered or missed, OneBy transcribes it, writes a summary, and creates and assigns the next task. We replace voicemail with an AI that acts.

## The Problem

For home services and property management, the phone is the cash register. That is also where the money leaks. Missed calls are missed leads, and a missed lead usually means the customer dials the next company on the list. Answered calls lose the details that matter (the address, the part, the promise to call back) because they live in someone's head or a sticky note. And the follow-ups that close jobs simply die: no one wrote them down, no one owned them. Voicemail makes it worse. It records a message that no one listens to in time, while the lead goes cold.

## What We Do

OneBy is the phone system and the AI in one product. Every call runs the same loop:

**Call -> AI -> Summary -> Task -> Resolution.**

The customer calls. The AI listens (on a desk phone or the mobile app, on answered calls and missed ones). It produces a clean summary of what was said and what was promised, then creates the follow-up task and assigns it to the right person. The owner sees outcomes, not a list of voicemails. Nothing falls through. That is the category we are building: communications that finish the job instead of just connecting the call.

## Why We Win

Three things have to be true at once, and only OneBy holds all three:

- **Communication-first.** We are the actual phone system, so we capture every call at the source. Workflow tools and CRMs never touch the call itself.
- **AI-native.** The AI is not a bolt-on. It runs on every call and turns conversation into structured action automatically.
- **Workflow-native.** We close the loop by creating and owning the task, not just storing a transcript.

Phone systems (RingCentral, OpenPhone, Dialpad, Zoom Phone) move dial tone but do not turn calls into action. Field and PM software (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, AppFolio) manages the job but is not the phone and never hears the call. CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce) store records but are heavy and not built around communication. OneBy sits at the center, built for the trades.

## Market

Home services (HVAC, plumbing, roofing) and property management are large, fragmented, and non-technical. These businesses live on the phone and lose real money to missed calls and forgotten details every day. Speed-to-lead decides who wins the job. Why now: AI voice and summarization are finally accurate enough to capture call outcomes automatically, without a human in the loop. The market has been underserved by tools that were either too generic or too complex.

## Business Model

Pricing is decoupled into lines plus AI seats, which matches how buyers already think and removes the main objection.

- **Basic Line (~$18/line):** calling, desk phone, SMS, and fax for any phone that just needs to dial.
- **AI Seat (~$89/seat):** the AI layer, added only on the lines that book jobs.
- **Common-area device (~$6):** shared phones at a low rate.

A 30-phone business does not pay a premium seat on every extension. This is land-and-expand: cheap lines get us in the door across the whole company, then AI seats expand onto the revenue-driving lines. AI stays the reason to choose OneBy.
