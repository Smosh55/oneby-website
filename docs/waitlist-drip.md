# Waitlist email drip (pre-launch)

Send from `leads@oneby.ai` via Resend (Broadcasts, or manually until volume
demands automation). Goal of the sequence: convert waitlist → founding member
before launch, and keep the list warm so launch day converts. Voice: same as
the blog — plainspoken, owner-to-owner, no hype. One CTA per email.

| # | When | Subject | Goal |
|---|------|---------|------|
| 1 | Immediately | You're in — here's your spot | Welcome + referral |
| 2 | Day 2 | What one missed call actually costs | Pain + calculator |
| 3 | Day 5 | Watch it answer a call (2-min demo) | Product proof |
| 4 | Day 8 | The founder rate, in plain numbers | Founders pitch |
| 5 | Day 14 | {N} spots left at the founder rate | Scarcity (only if true) |
| 6 | Launch week | Doors open {date} — you're first | Launch |

is roughly $80k a year walking to the competitor down the road. Framed as an
example — but run your own numbers: oneby.ai/missed-call-calculator

The fix isn't answering more. It's a phone that answers itself, writes down
what the caller needed, and puts the job on your board.

That's what we're launching in August. Your spot's saved.
